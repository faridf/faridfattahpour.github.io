Projects

  • Resiliency and Morphology of High-Entropy Oxide Material: Predicted crystal morphology using the Wulff construction. Developed models to understand the structural transformations under varying conditions.
  • Heat Radiation Mitigation in Pyrosilicate Composites: Modeled heat radiation effects and refractive index properties in rare-earth pyrosilicate composites using DFT.
  • Improving Ab Initio Diffusion Calculations: Applied machine learning techniques for predicting diffusion in materials and locating transition states. Improved accuracy of diffusion rate predictions in complex structures.
  • Understanding Anharmonic Phonons in BCC Metals: Performed DFT simulations to understand the role of anharmonic phonons in the diffusion of BCC metals. Utilized s-TDEP Method, Phonopy, and c-NEB Method.
  • Thermoplastic Polyurethane Synthesis: Synthesized a novel polyurethane with controllable degradation properties for biomedical applications. Conducted organic synthesis of zwitterionic molecules and synthesized novel CB diol.
  • Investigation of Nanosilica Effect on PVDF Nanofibers: Fabricated PVDF nanofibers with incorporated nanosilica using polymer blending and electrospinning, enhancing mechanical and piezoelectric properties.
  • Preparation of MoS2 Nanosheets Using Oxidation: Synthesized MoS2 nanosheets using acid treatment, ultrasonication, oxidation with novel oxidants, and thermal shocking to significantly increase surface area and enhance catalytic properties.
